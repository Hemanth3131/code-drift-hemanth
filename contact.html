Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For popular destinations during peak season, we recommend booking 6-8 months in advance to secure the best accommodations and prices. For off-season travel, 3-4 months advance booking is usually sufficient. Last-minute bookings are possible but may have limited options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For certain destinations, we also offer installment payment plans to help you budget for your dream vacation.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows for a full refund if canceled 60 days before departure, a 50% refund if canceled 30-59 days before departure, and no refund for cancellations less than 30 days before departure. We recommend purchasing travel insurance for unexpected circumstances.

Do you arrange travel insurance?

Yes, we can arrange comprehensive travel insurance that covers trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and other unexpected events. While not mandatory, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all international trips.

How do I know if I need a visa for my destination?

Visa requirements vary based on your nationality and destination. During the booking process, we'll provide information about visa requirements for your specific situation. We can also assist with visa application guidance, though the actual application process remains the traveler's responsibility.
